Installations

Premium Installations. Real Warranties.

Tile, flooring, waterproofing, and shower construction — using approved systems and backed by a 3-year workmanship guarantee.

01
01 — Tile

Tile Installation

Floor tile, wall tile, shower tile, backsplash, fireplace surrounds, and exterior. Large format, small format, offset patterns, mitered corners — set correctly, grouted with Mapei, and built to last.

02
02 — Flooring

Hardwood & Vinyl Flooring

Engineered hardwood, LVP, and SPC flooring installed with proper underlayment and subfloor preparation. Every install starts with a flat, solid foundation.

03
03 — Waterproofing

Waterproofing Systems

Shower pans, pre-slopes, liner membranes, and full waterproofing assemblies using Mapei, Schluter, Prova, and Wedi systems. If it gets wet, it's waterproofed — properly.

04
04 — Showers

Shower Construction

Full shower builds from the pan up. Pre-slope, waterproofing membrane, wall tile, floor tile, niche, bench, trim, and glass prep — all in one scope, sequenced properly, and done right.

Approved Products Only

Systems That Perform

We work with Mapei, Schluter, Prova, and Wedi systems across all installations. Non-approved products void our warranty — and we'll tell you that upfront.
