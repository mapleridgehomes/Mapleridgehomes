Renovations

Kitchen Renovations

Layout changes, tile backsplashes, hardwood or vinyl flooring, and finish work. We coordinate all trades and sequence the work properly — so your kitchen doesn't sit half-finished for three weeks waiting on one decision.

Bathroom Renovations

Full bathroom builds, shower tile, waterproofing, flooring, and trim. Every shower we build includes professional-grade waterproofing using Mapei, Schluter, Prova, or Wedi systems — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

Basement Renovations

Flooring, framing, tile, and full finish work. We turn unfinished space into livable, properly built rooms — with the same level of quality and planning we bring to any job above grade.
