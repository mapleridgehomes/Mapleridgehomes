Our Process

Think Through to the Finish.

A disciplined process from day one — so nothing gets missed and nothing gets rushed.

05
Five Steps

How Every Project Runs

01

Consultation

We start with your goals, timeline, and budget — so expectations are aligned before any work begins. We ask the right questions upfront so nothing important gets missed down the road. No obligation, no pressure.

02

Scope & Plan

We define the full scope in detail, confirm all materials and specifications, and build a clear plan before anything is ordered or scheduled. The scope is what we price, and what we deliver.

03

Quote & Schedule

You receive a clear, itemized quote — no vague estimates. We lock in the schedule, confirm sequencing, and ensure all decisions are made before work starts.

04

Build & Updates

We manage the work with consistent communication. You're kept informed throughout — not left wondering. If something changes, you hear about it from us before it becomes a problem.

05

Final Walkthrough

We complete a full walkthrough together, address any punch-list items, and close out the project properly. Every job ends with a signed-off, clean result — and a copy of your warranty.

Every installation is backed by our 3-Year Workmanship Warranty — using Mapei, Schluter, Prova, and Wedi approved systems. That's not a promise. It's in writing.
