We’re Hiring for Two Roles

Carpenter / Foreman

Full Time Simcoe County, ON

About the Role

We're looking for an experienced carpenter who can handle both exterior and interior finishing work — and who's ready to take on a leadership role. You'll be running crews on active job sites, maintaining Maple Ridge's standard of quality, and working directly alongside Mitchell on builds across Simcoe County and beyond.

This isn't a position for someone who needs hand-holding. We need someone who shows up, solves problems, and holds their crew to the same standard we hold ourselves to.

What We're Looking For

  • Exterior and interior finishing experience — you know both sides
  • Ability to run and coordinate crews independently
  • Strong attention to detail and finish quality
  • Own tools preferred — not a dealbreaker if not
  • Red Seal not required — experience and attitude matter more
  • Valid driver's licence

Compensation

$25 – $45/hour depending on experience and skillset

Office Administrator

Part Time / Remote Remote — Simcoe County preferred

About the Role

Maple Ridge Homes is a growing construction company and we need someone sharp to help keep the back end running clean. This role supports Mitchell directly — managing scheduling, keeping email organized and responded to, tracking quotes and projects, and handling the day-to-day admin that keeps a busy trades business moving.

You'll be working remotely, 35 hours a week, and you'll need to be someone who stays ahead of things without being asked twice.

What We're Looking For

  • Organized, detail-oriented, and self-directed
  • Strong written communication — you'll be representing the brand in emails and client-facing correspondence
  • Comfortable with scheduling, inbox management, and document organization
  • Experience with QuickBooks Online (QBO) is a strong asset
  • Notion or similar project management tool experience helpful
  • Construction or trades industry background is a bonus, not a requirement

Compensation

Competitive — based on experience. Let's talk.
