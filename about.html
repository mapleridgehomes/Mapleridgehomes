Our Story

Who We Are

Maple Ridge Homes was built on a simple principle: do the work right, every time. I'm Mitchell Flach — the owner and lead installer behind every project we take on.

Construction runs in my family. With 35+ years of family building experience, I grew up on job sites learning from people who took real pride in their craft. Today, I bring that same standard to every tile, floor, and installation we complete.

We're not a large crew sending strangers to your home. When you hire Maple Ridge, you work directly with me — from the first call to the final walkthrough.
